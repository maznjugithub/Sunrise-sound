About Us

AVL Solutions for Unforgettable Events

Sunrise Sound Pvt. Ltd. is a professional event support and audiovisual service provider based in kathmandu, Nepal. The company specializes in delivering complete technical solutions for events of all scales, ensuring high-quality sound, lighting, visuals, and stage setups. With a strong commitment to reliability, creativity, and customer satisfaction, Sunrise Sound Service Pvt.Ltd. has established itself as a trusted partner for corporate events, social functions, concerts, weddings, exhibitions, and cultural programs.

Founded by Raj Shrestha, a veteran with over 20 years of hands-on industry experience. We are the only AVL company in Nepal to hold ISO 9001:2015 certification — your guarantee of quality and reliability.

🏆 ISO 9001:2015 Certified

The only certified AVL company in Nepal, meeting international quality standards.

⚙️ CE-Certified Equipment

In-house certified sound, lighting and LED systems — zero third-party dependencies.

📐 Precision Planning

Every event is previsualized, pre-planned, and executed with strict safety measures.

🌍 Global Experience
